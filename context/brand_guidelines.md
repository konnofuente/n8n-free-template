# Geskap Brand Guidelines

## Identity
- **Product:** Geskap — All-in-one business management platform for African SMEs
- **Company:** CamaireTech
- **Tagline:** "Gérez toute votre entreprise depuis un seul écran."

## Website & Social Media
- **Website:** https://geskap.com/
- **Instagram:** @geskap_app — https://www.instagram.com/geskap_app
- **TikTok:** @geskap_app — https://www.tiktok.com/@geskap_app
- **Facebook:** Geskap — https://www.facebook.com/share/1MZFJfrJ6q/

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** #194E2F (Deep African Green)
- **Font:** Aclonica
- **Logo:** Always use on white or dark background — never on busy backgrounds

## Tone of Voice
- **Direct** — Get to the point immediately. No fluff.
- **Practical** — Always show real benefit, not features
- **African-first** — Written by Africans, for Africans. Never generic.
- **Empowering** — We believe in the African entrepreneur

## Language Rules

### Always Say
- "gérez votre business"
- "en temps réel"
- "depuis un seul écran"
- "pour les entrepreneurs africains"
- "votre caissier ne peut pas..."
- "vous savez exactement..."

### Never Say
- "synergy"
- "leverage"
- "solution"
- "world-class"
- "cutting-edge"
- "disruptive"

## Content Pillars
1. **Anti-theft** — Employee theft protection, audit trail, permissions
2. **Mobile-first** — Works on any phone, no internet required
3. **African payments** — MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money natively
4. **Real profit** — Know your real numbers, not estimates
5. **All-in-one** — No more notebooks, spreadsheets, WhatsApp chaos

## Personas (Priority Order)
1. Boutique/shop owner worried about employee theft
2. Restaurant owner managing WhatsApp orders
3. Small manufacturer tracking production costs
4. Wholesaler managing supplier and customer debts
5. Multi-branch business owner needing remote visibility

## Competitors We Beat
- Excel/Google Sheets → No real-time stock, no POS, no African payments
- Paper/notebooks → No audit trail, easy to manipulate
- Global ERPs (SAP, Odoo) → Too expensive, too complex, not African
