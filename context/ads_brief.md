# Geskap Facebook Ads Brief

## Budget
- **Monthly budget:** 50,000 CFA/month
- **Recommended split:** 70% conversion ads / 30% awareness ads

## Target Countries (Priority Order)
1. 🇨🇲 Cameroon (primary — Douala & Yaoundé first)
2. 🇨🇮 Côte d'Ivoire (Abidjan)
3. 🇹🇬 Togo (Lomé)
4. 🇨🇩 DRC (Kinshasa, Lubumbashi)
5. All Francophone Africa (broader awareness)

## Campaign Goals
- **Primary:** App signups / account creation on geskap.com
- **Secondary:** Brand awareness among SME owners
- **Tertiary:** Demo requests / WhatsApp inquiries

## Target Audiences

### Audience 1 — Shop & Boutique Owners (Highest Priority)
- Age: 25–45
- Gender: All
- Interests: Small business, retail, entrepreneurship, mobile money
- Pain: Employee theft, no financial visibility
- Language: French

### Audience 2 — Restaurant & Food Business Owners
- Age: 25–50
- Interests: Restaurant management, food delivery, WhatsApp Business
- Pain: Order chaos, ingredient tracking, unknown profit
- Language: French

### Audience 3 — Manufacturers & Artisans
- Age: 25–50
- Interests: Production, wholesale, raw materials
- Pain: Unknown production costs, raw material tracking
- Language: French

### Audience 4 — Anglophone Africa (Secondary)
- Countries: Cameroon (Anglophone regions)
- Language: English
- Same business profiles as above

## Ad Formats to Use
- **Video ads** (best for TikTok-style content — show the app in action)
- **Carousel ads** (showcase multiple features)
- **Single image ads** (bold text + one strong pain point)
- **Lead generation ads** (collect WhatsApp numbers directly)

## Key Messages Per Audience

### For Shop Owners
> "Votre caissier encaisse — mais tout arrive dans votre caisse? Avec Geskap, chaque vente est enregistrée en temps réel. Personne ne peut vous voler sans que vous le sachiez."

### For Restaurant Owners
> "Les commandes WhatsApp, les dépenses, les ingrédients — tout dans un seul écran. Gérez votre restaurant depuis votre téléphone."

### For Manufacturers
> "Vous savez exactement combien vous coûte chaque produit que vous fabriquez? Avec Geskap, le coût de production est calculé automatiquement."

## Landing Page
- All ads point to: **https://geskap.com/**
- CTA: "Commencer gratuitement" / "Créer mon compte"

## What NOT to Do in Ads
- No stock photos of non-African people
- No English-only ads for Francophone markets
- No technical jargon
- No long paragraphs — short, punchy sentences only
