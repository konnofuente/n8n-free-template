## Target Audience on Social Media
- African SME owners aged 25–45
- Primarily Francophone Africa (French content = priority)
- Mobile-first users — most see content on their phones
- Active on WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (Rotate Weekly)
1. **Education** — How to use a Geskap feature (tutorial style)
2. **Pain Point** — Call out a real problem African entrepreneurs face
3. **Social Proof** — Real user stories, testimonials, results
4. **Product Demo** — Show the app working in real time
5. **Motivation** — Entrepreneurship content that builds community

## Content Formats Per Platform

### TikTok
- **Best format:** Screen recordings of Geskap in action (30–60 seconds)
- **Hook style:** Start with the pain — "Tu sais combien tu as vraiment gagné aujourd'hui?"
- **Trending:** Duets with entrepreneur content, POV videos, before/after

### Instagram
- **Feed:** Clean graphics with bold French text + #194E2F green branding
- **Reels:** Same as TikTok content repurposed
- **Stories:** Polls ("Tu utilises encore un cahier?"), Q&A, feature highlights
- **Carousel:** "5 raisons pour lesquelles ton employé te vole sans que tu le saches"

### Facebook
- **Best format:** Longer posts with storytelling + image or video
- **Groups:** Post in Cameroonian entrepreneur groups, African business groups
- **Ads:** Conversion-focused (see ads_brief.md)
